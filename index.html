Our Story

Authentic Stories,
Intimate Celebrations

The Kahani Crew is an event management company based in Hyderabad, specializing in intimate and farmhouse weddings. Our philosophy revolves around crafting bespoke experiences that reflect your unique love story.

We believe that your wedding shouldn't just be an event; it should be a narrative. A "Kahani" that breathes through every detail, every smile, and every silent look shared between you.
