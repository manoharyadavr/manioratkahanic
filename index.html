Our Story

Authentic Stories,
Intimate Celebrations

The Kahani Crew is an event management company based in Hyderabad, specializing in intimate and farmhouse weddings. Our philosophy revolves around crafting bespoke experiences that reflect your unique love story.

We believe that your wedding shouldn't just be an event; it should be a narrative. A "Kahani" that breathes through every detail, every smile, and every silent look shared between you.

Our Philosophy

What Makes Us Unique

At The Kahani Crew, we believe every wedding is a narrative waiting to be told with elegance and intimacy.

Intimate Weddings

We prioritize depth over distance, focusing on small-scale, meaningful gatherings that resonate with authentic emotion and personal connection.

Farmhouse & Resorts

Specializing in the rustic charm and luxury of Hyderabad's best retreats, we transform open spaces into bespoke cinematic experiences.

Modern Minimalist

Elegance through simplicity. We believe less is often significantly more, focusing on curated details that make a massive visual impact.

Storytelling Photography

Candid moments captured with an eye for authenticity. We don't just take pictures; we document the soul of your Kahani.
